MTF vs FTM Lag Comparison
========================================
MTF (n=31): mean lag = 1.710
FTM (n=36): mean lag = 0.778

Welch t-test: t=1.2665, p=0.2108
Mann-Whitney: U=618, p=0.4468

Interpretation: p<0.05 means MTF and FTM show statistically
different lead times, suggesting gender-direction matters.
